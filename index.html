Moonlit Mess

it's quiet here

you can leave what you're carrying

Some things need somewhere to go.
Leave yours here.

what you write here never leaves this page.

Leave something soft
for a stranger to find tonight.

only this part is shared — anonymously, always.

Someone will find this.
Thank you for leaving something kind behind.

someone left this for you
